How to Make Double Fried Lemon Pepper Chicken

Here are the steps on how to make double fried lemon pepper chicken:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:<

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and cayenne pepper.
  2. In a shallow dish, whisk together the buttermilk and egg.
  3. In a third bowl, combine the panko bread crumbs.
  4. Dredge the chicken pieces in the flour mixture, then dip in the buttermilk mixture, and finally coat in the panko bread crumbs.
  5. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  6. Fry the chicken in batches until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes per batch.
  7. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside on a paper towel-lined plate to drain.
  8. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, lemon zest, salt, and pepper.
  9. Drizzle the lemon juice mixture over the chicken and serve immediately.

Tips:

  • You can use any type of chicken you like, such as boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs.
  • If you don't have buttermilk, you can make your own by combining 1 cup of milk with 1 tablespoon of white vinegar or lemon juice.
  • You can add any other herbs or spices to the breading, such as dried oregano, thyme, or rosemary.
  • You can serve the chicken with your favorite dipping sauce, such as ranch dressing, blue cheese dressing, or honey mustard sauce.
  • Enjoy!
